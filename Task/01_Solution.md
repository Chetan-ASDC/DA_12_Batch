# Solutions of Practice Tasks – Descriptive Statistics  
## PART A: Ungrouped Data

**Note:**  
- Mean = Sum of observations / Number of observations  
- Median = Middle value after arranging data in ascending order  
- Mode = Most frequently occurring value  
- Range = Highest value − Lowest value  
- Standard Deviation (Population)  
  σ² = Σ(x − x̄)² / N  
  σ = √σ²  

---

## Task 1: Mean
**Data:** 2, 4, 6, 8, 10  

Sum = 2 + 4 + 6 + 8 + 10 = 30  
Number of observations (N) = 5  

**Mean = 30 / 5 = 6**

## Task 6: Mean and Standard Deviation
**Data:** 7, 14, 21, 28  

Mean (x̄) = (7 + 14 + 21 + 28) / 4 = **17.5**

| x  | x − x̄ | (x − x̄)² |
|----|-------|-----------|
| 7  | -10.5 | 110.25 |
| 14 | -3.5  | 12.25  |
| 21 | 3.5   | 12.25  |
| 28 | 10.5  | 110.25 |

Σ(x − x̄)² = 245  

σ² = 245 / 4 = 61.25  
σ = √61.25 ≈ **7.83**
