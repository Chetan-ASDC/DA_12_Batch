##  Tasks

###  Basic SUM Tasks
1. Find the **total sales amount**.  
2. Find the **total quantity sold**.  

---

###  SUMIF / SUMIFS Tasks
3. Find the **total sales in the West region**.  
4. Find the **total sales amount for Mobile products**.  
5. Find the **total sales in North region for Laptop products**.  

---

###  AVERAGE Tasks
6. Find the **average sales amount**.  
7. Find the **average quantity sold per order**.  

---

###  AVERAGEIF / AVERAGEIFS Tasks
8. Find the **average sales amount in the East region**.  

---

###  COUNT Tasks
9. Count the **total number of orders**.  
10. Count how many orders are from **West region with sales above 30,000**.  

---
